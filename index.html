How Does CareConnect Work?

Getting help is easy. Just follow these 3 simple steps — no login, no fees, no paperwork.

1

Fill the Form

Tell us your name, location, and health concern. Takes less than 2 minutes.

2

We Match a Volunteer

Our system finds the right volunteer — a doctor, nurse, or counselor near you.

3

Get Help Within 24 Hrs

Our volunteer contacts you by phone. Get guidance, referrals, or direct medical help.

What We Offer

All services are 100% free. We are a non-profit NGO helping communities access healthcare.

Patient Support

Submit your health concern. We connect you with a nearby doctor or healthcare volunteer — completely free.

AI Health Assistant

Click the chat button (bottom-right) to instantly ask health questions. Available 24/7, no signup needed.

Volunteer Matching

Register as a volunteer. We match your skills (doctor, nurse, counselor, tech) with patients who need you.

Smart Data Summary

Every request is auto-summarised for NGO coordinators, helping them track patient needs and allocate resources faster.
